#### What do I mean by the Wisdom of the Elders?

I'll keep it simple. The tragedy of man is that as soon as he is built he is knocked down. In other words, by the time you are wise enough to see through this game you are in old age and about to die. This is like building a house and as soon as it is built knocking it down and starting to build a new one and doing that over and over again for generations. So, I know "you can't put an old head on young shoulders", but, I think there is a ton of waste the way that life builds a man up to the point of being wise and then he dies and all that wisdom is lost and life has to start from blank ignorance again (a new born in other words). Arresting  this wastage involves consulting the elders and what this involves is them writing their life stories and what they learnt in them or through them and the lessons they got. And that is what this is about. If you are willing to write your life story and put it in a book, then, I will link to it from here and it can be passed on to the youth. For a lot of older people, when the young ones are going through life it is transparent how it will work out for them because the older people had an equivalent experience when they were young. So, if you are elderly why not write up what your life has taught you and do something about not letting it go to waste. Wiser cultures of the past used to mandate that young people listened to the elders of the community but the brash society we live in now has largely dispensed with that and I think it needs to be brought back because the world is a paradox right now in that its very clever but very naive at the same time.
