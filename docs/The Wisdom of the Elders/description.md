#### What do I mean by the Wisdom of the Elders?

The tragedy of man is that as soon as he is built he is knocked down. In other words, by the time you are wise enough to see through this game you are in old age and about to die. This is like building a house and as soon as it is built knocking it down and starting to build a new one and doing that over and over again for generations and maybe, perversely that is what is happening in our world. So, I know "you can't put an old head on young shoulders", but, I think there is a ton of waste the way that life builds a man up to the point of being wise and then he dies and all that wisdom is lost and life has to start from blank ignorance again (a new born in other words). Arresting  this wastage involves consulting the elders and the lessons they learnt over the course of a lifetime. And that is what this is about. If you are willing to write your life story and put it in a book, then, I will link to it from here and it can be passed on to the youth. For a lot of older people, what the young ones are going through in life is transparent in terms of how it will work out for them because the older people had an equivalent experience when they were young. So, if you are elderly why not write up what your life has taught you and do something about not letting it go to waste. Wiser cultures of the past used to mandate that young people listened to the elders of the community but the brash society we live in now has largely dispensed with that and I think it needs to be brought back because the world is a paradox right now in that it's very clever but very naive at the same time.  

Another thing I want to say is that like many men find a woman more attractive with no makeup on because it's more honest I will find anything you write about your life experience much more genuine if you don't have it edited by an editor in the interest of making yourself look more polished and clean than you really are, or, it could be said, "in putting forward a false image of oneself".  In other words, as the Nirvana song recommends "Come as you are" or as I say, "Don't try to make yourself look different to how you actually are using deceptions". We live in a world now where mostly everything appears different to how it really is, its dishonest it makes a mess and it is a disease. In other words, what I am looking for is simple, if you can't spell, don't spell anything else and we begin to lose our authenticity or what we authentically or genuinely are as people which is a terrible price if you really understood what you are losing for what seems like a quick win. Something that the wiser elders will know already is that there's no such thing as a quick win that doesn't also shoot you in the foot at the same time and that's what I would claim will happen with the AI hype like all other quick wins in life. Elders will tell the young a simple truth, "if it's easy, it's crap". The key to heaven is simple, "honesty" and condemnation to hell is simple also, "dishonesty" and if you have your work edited to make it look different to how it is or you are that is a step towards dishonest hell and away from honest heaven and so if that's what you want "go for it" I ain't gonna stop you but unless we start getting more genuine when we are on a path of becoming more and more disingenuous to make a quick buck we have got some work to do. If you are elderly please understand you actually have more to offer than you ever did before, you just have to be able to see and action it that is all.

If you wanted to write your life experiences and your life lessons, which is what I am looking for here, its not a huge task you could write 1 page a day and in a year you have got 365 pages or half a page a day and in 2 years you have got 365 pages. Maybe you have a diary or a journal that you have kept that you could précis or condense as a learning aid for the youth. Who knows maybe a body of work can be forged between us (the elders) that can make the lessons of people who are not living in an ivory tower available so that the same snares can't be set for the new kids as they were for us when we were kids. I ain't doing this on my own if I am to write up what I have learned in my life, then, I stipulate that others must be writing up what they have learned also and so if they aren't  or won't that puts the all round kibosh on it. It might be contentious what I am suggesting here because the way you learn to ride a bike is by riding the bike, falling off and learning not to fall off, but, that's a very hamfisted way of going about life and when its done en masse like that it makes a hell of a mess. So, I think a few lessons from older guys with the wisdom of their decades behind them might be a real education for the youth not the hamfisted crap that you get filled up with by the indoctrination system that mascerades as an education system.
